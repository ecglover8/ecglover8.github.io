My name is Gene and Gigi Web & Graphic Design is my custom website design and development company. I started this business in October of 2017 while working on a full stack web development program. I actively looking for clients among small and large organizations that are looking to create or enhance their web presence. No project is too big or too small. I am based in Austin, TX but am available to work with anyone, anywhere. Except in outer space, it is way too cold there.

Gigi Web & Graphic Design is focused on creating and deploying dynamic web applications utilizing the following technologies:

  • HTML/CSS
  • PHP
  • Javascript libraries, including
    • D3
    • jQuery
    • React
  • NodeJS
  • Ruby
  • SQL and noSQL Databases, including
    • mySQL
    • MongoDB
    • MariaDB
    • PostgreSQL

My philosophy here is that less is always more, unless you are talking about ice cream. If you can create an effect using just HTML and CSS, just use HTML and CSS. Make every effect be as fancy as it needs to be, and no fancier. Keep outside libraries and loading times to a minimum. Put your most important stuff up front, and less important stuff further down the page.
